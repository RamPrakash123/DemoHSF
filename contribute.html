HSF Special Campaign

Shanti Kumar a degree student met with a terrible accident. He is on the ventilator fighting to recover.

  • Shanti Kumar met with a terrible accident on 20th August 2019 early in the morning at Tarnaka while crossing the road. He was hit by a bus and the bus went over him. His skin on the back is all peeled off. The front tires of the bus went over his ribs fracturing them.
  • The broken ribs pierced the internal organs and Shanti Kumar was unable to move an inch. He was immediately shifted to the hospital and doctors have done a quick first aid.
  • But due the accident, Shanti Kumar is on ventilator and is fighting to live. The damaged organs had to be removed as they were developing infection in his body. The further treatment will cost them 40-60 thousand per day. The total estimate is about 7-8 lakhs for the complete treatment and operation.
  • The family is in a helpless situation where they are not able to afford the treatment nor they could lose their only son Shanti Kumar.
  • Shanti Kumar’s father is requesting you to please come forward and extend your support in saving Shanti Kumar’s life.
